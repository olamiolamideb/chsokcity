About us

History

In December 1990,during a two week intensive spiritual excercise of fasting and prayer, Word Integrity Ministries christ House church Internatinal was received as a ministry assignment in a deep encounter with the Holy Spirit. The church was inaugurated in January 1991 in sokoto North-West of Nigeria

With a total of 12 persons, comprising 8 adult and 4 children in the inaugural service under the dynamic leadership of Dr. moses John Akor. ChristHouse Church is an Apostolic and prohetic church - based Christian ministry characterised with signs, wonders and miracles with branches around Nigeria

Our Vision

Guiding individuals, regardless of gender, culture, background, or belief, to experience God's glory and positively impact our community and generation through a deep understanding of the Word of God. This leads to a personal encounter with Christ and empowers them to embody the character of the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture individuals for a life of supernatural living and global leadership, emphasizing the final harvest in anticipation of the Lord's return
