About Us

In December 1990,during a two week intensive spiritual excercise of fasting and prayer, Word Integrity Ministries christ House church Internatinal was received as a ministry assignment in a deep encounter with the Holy Spirit. The church was inaugurated in January 1991 in sokoto North-West of Nigeria With a total of 12 persons, comprising 8 adult and 4 children in the inaugural service under the dynamic leadership of Dr. moses John Akor. ChristHouse Church is an Apostolic and prohetic church - based Christian ministry characterised with signs, wonders and miracles with branches around Nigeria
